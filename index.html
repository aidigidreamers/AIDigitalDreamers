Web Based Institute Management System

Digitize Your Schools to Compete the World

IMS is a web-based application that provides facilities of Digitizing the education system From Registration to issuing leaving certificate Planning for exams to creating result cards Fee collection to generating payslips Everything is digitalized.

IMS is an application that makes the head of an institute able to check and manage all the activities like student & staff attendance, Fee collection, Staff Salary, Results, Announcements, and everything that takes place in an institute, from anywhere in the whole world.

This application has an attractive and User-Friendly interface. Using that head of an institute will also be able to allow or cancel the roles to the other users of that application in his/her institute.
